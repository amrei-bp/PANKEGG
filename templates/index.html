About Pankegg

Learn more about the project and its objectives

Our Mission

Pankegg aims to provide a comprehensive tool for the analysis and visualization of metabolic pathways across various samples and bins. Our goal is to facilitate research and understanding in the field of metagenomics and bioinformatics.

Features and Benefits

With Pankegg, you can:

  • Compare samples and bins with ease
  • Visualize taxonomic compositions
  • Analyze pathway completions
  • Export data for further analysis
